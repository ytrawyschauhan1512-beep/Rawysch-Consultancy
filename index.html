About Rawysch Consultancy

Rawysch Consultancy is a professional job consultancy dedicated to connecting talented individuals with genuine career opportunities. Founded with the vision to bridge the gap between talent and opportunity, we work with complete trust, transparency, and teamwork.

Our journey started with a simple idea helping job seekers connect directly with verified companies, without middlemen or fake promises. What began as a small WhatsApp group called "Work From Job" in early 2024, has now grown into a community of 1,000+ active candidates across India.

What We Do

Connect job seekers directly with verified company HRs.

Provide complete placement support from profile screening to interview preparation.

Partner with trusted companies to help them hire qualified and reliable candidates.

Offer genuine job updates and assessments through our growing online community.

Our Founders Journey

The Beginning

The story of Rawysch Consultancy started in 2019, when our founder began his career at a travel company in Delhi. Those early months taught valuable lessons in teamwork, professionalism, and ground-level operations.

The Idea is Born

In 2020, a conversation with a consultancy sparked a new idea to help job seekers connect directly with companies and earn through successful placements. That moment planted the seed for what would later become Rawysch Consultancy.

Experience & Growth

While working at Amazon for nearly two years, our founder continued consultancy as a side project, learning about hiring trends, market demand, and how to build strong professional networks. This blend of corporate experience and passion for connecting people formed the base of today's company.

Overcoming Challenges

When he decided to pursue consultancy full-time, many discouraged him but he kept going. Days were for the job, nights for consultancy. This relentless effort turned a small idea into a growing business.

Partnership & Growth

In July 2024, he met Gaurav, who believed in the vision and decided to join as a partner. Together, they gave the business structure and scale.

  • Gaurav now handles direct communication and tie-ups with company HR teams
  • The Founder manages candidate interaction, shortlisting, and placement processes

This partnership gave our work a more professional and scalable structure.

Vision Ahead

Rawysch Consultancy is more than just a consultancy it's a platform built on courage, trust, and hard work. Our founders' journey proves that with dedication and belief, even a small idea can grow into something meaningful.

Our Goal: To make Rawysch Consultancy India's most trusted name in job consultancy where every candidate finds the right opportunity, and every company finds the right talent.

Our Vision

"To bridge the gap between Talent & Opportunity with trust, transparency, and teamwork."

Our focus is not just on placements, but on building careers. Rawysch Consultancy's vision is to ensure that every deserving youth gets the right opportunity.
